Quesadilla

Home Page

Description:

This is a step by step on how to make a Quesadilla.

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese

Step-By-Step:

  1. Turn stove top and place pan
  2. Place tortilla then put cheese on half
  3. Fold tortilla over and let cook for 5 min on med temp
  4. Flip tortilla to other side and let cook for another 5 min
  5. Eat
