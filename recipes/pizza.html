Description:

This is a step by step on how to make a Pizza.

Ingredients:
  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Protein
  • Veggies

Step-By-Step:

  1. Turn Oven on to 420 degree
  2. Flatten dough to 18 in
  3. Put sauce then toppings
  4. Put in oven for 20 min or until crust is light brown
  5. Slice and eat
