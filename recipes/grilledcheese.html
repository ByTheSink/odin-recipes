Grilled Cheese

Home Page

Description:

This is a step by step on how to make a Grilled Cheese.

Ingredients:
  • Bread
  • cheese slice

Step-By-Step:

  1. Turn Stove top on and place pan on burner.
  2. Put cheese slice between two bread slices
  3. Put combined sandwich on pan
  4. Flip after 5 minutes and let cook for another 5min
  5. Remove grilled cheese and place on plate
  6. Enjoy
